Why Gold? Understanding Gold as a Safe Haven Asset

For over 5,000 years, gold has been a trusted store of value globally. In India, it carries profound cultural significance, central to weddings, festivals, and intergenerational wealth transfer. Beyond its cultural importance, gold is a powerful financial instrument.

Inflation Hedge
Gold preserves purchasing power against inflation and economic instability.
Portfolio Diversification
Low correlation to equities offers stability, reducing portfolio risk during volatility.
Cultural Wealth
In India, gold signifies prosperity, security, and intergenerational wealth.
